## Purpose

security practices against ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements, identifying 
areas of compliance and gaps requiring remediation.

## Key Recommendations

1. **Immediate Priority** — Establish a formal Information Security Policy
2. **Immediate Priority** — Develop and maintain a Risk Register
3. **Short Term** — Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
4. **Short Term** — Launch a Security Awareness Training programme
5. **Medium Term** — Establish an Internal Audit programme
6. **Medium Term** — Define measurable Information Security Objectives
7. **Medium Term** — Conduct formal Management Review meetings

---

## Conclusion
SecureShop LLC has significant gaps across most ISO/IEC 27001:2022 
clauses, particularly in risk management, performance evaluation, and 
formal documentation. Immediate action is recommended to establish 
foundational ISMS controls before pursuing certification.
